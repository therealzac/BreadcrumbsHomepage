Deleting Your Data

To delete your data on Breadcrumbs, first navigate to the "sources" view in the app.

Select the source you'd like to delete, and select "Disconnect Source".

You will see a confirmation popup to confirm if you're sure about deleting your data. Select "Yes".

Aaaand it's gone!
