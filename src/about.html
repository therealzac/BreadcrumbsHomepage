We are creating an internet where you own your data.

It's no secret tech companies control the world's data. We don't think that's fair. Breadcrumbs was created to help people secure, own, and capitalize on their personal data.

We see a future of privacy, where the benefits of data's intrinsic value is shared among those who own it, and not concentrated in the hands of a few large companies.

Our plan.

By providing the world's best data security services, we're fostering a user base to launch a personal data market where you can choose to sell your data at your discretion. You will be able to buy and sell data as an individual or organization on a stock market of information.

We believe this new type of data economy is both fair and inevitable. It's in our best interest to make sure your data is as secure as possible, so an alignment of interest ensures data security policies are enforced on the internet at large. It's your data. Take control.
