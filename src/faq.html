About Breadcrumbs.

Why did we decide to found Breadcrumbs?

The internet is really only 30 years old, and there are a number of critical changes we believe are inevitable to make the internet a safer and more useful tool. One of these changes is a shift towards users' awareness and control of their data, which has been happening for a number of years already. We think it's important that users understand the power of their own data and what can be ascertained from it, and it's a mission that drives us to make a massive positive impact on the world.

What is Breadcrumbs?

Breadcrumbs allows users to secure, own, and capitalize on their personal data. It's a suite of software products that provides users with state-of-the-art privacy technology while simultaneously allowing them to view and analyze the data that's being protected.

With Breadcrumbs you can:

  • Secure your digital activity with a decentralized VPN.
  • Find out which companies currently hold your data and reclaim it.
  • Monitor security risks like credit card fraud and data breaches.
  • Collect and analyze your own data and know yourself better than Google does.
  • Get paid to sell your data on our data marketplace.

    • What is a decentralized VPN?

    A decentralized VPN (dVPN) is a virtual private network service that runs on a blockchain, making it qualitatively more secure than a centralized VPN. Standard VPN providers may store logs of your internet traffic (even if they say they don't) because all of your activity is routed through one of their servers. A dVPN distributes your internet traffic through a peer-to-peer network of thousands of nodes, so your activity inherently cannot be tracked.
